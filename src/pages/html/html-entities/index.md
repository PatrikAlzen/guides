Charachter entities are used to display charachters which are reserved in HTML.

You can write a charachter entity in one of two ways:
 - &entity_name;
 - &#entity_number;

# Common Charachter Entities

Charachter | Name | Entity Name | Entity Number
-----------|------|-------------|--------------
&nbsp;  | non-breaking space | `&nbsp;` | `&#160;`
& | ampersand | `&amp;` | `&#38;`
¢ | cent | `&cent;` | `&#162;`
€ | euro | `&euro;` | `&#8364;`
£ | pound | `&pound;` | `&#163;`
¥ | yen | `&yen;` | `&#165;`
© | copyright | `&copy;` | `&#169;`
' | single quotation mark | `&apos;` | `&#39;`
" | double quotation mark | `&quot;` | `&#34;`
`>` | greater than | `&gt;` | `&#62;`
`<` | less than | `&lt;` | `&#60;`
® | registered trademark | `&reg;` | `&#174;`
